Our Story

About Duwakot FoodLand

Founded in Duwakot, Bhaktapur, Duwakot FoodLand started as a humble neighbourhood eatery serving authentic Nepali snacks and meals. Over the years, we've grown into a full-service dining destination — spanning a kitchen, a bar, a café, and Nepal's favourite auto spa, all under one roof.

Our menu celebrates the richness of Nepali cuisine — from sekuwa and momo to biryanis and continental sizzlers — alongside an extensive drinks menu and café offerings.

Policies

Last updated: January 2025

Privacy Policy

We collect only the information you provide when making a booking or placing an order: your name, phone number, and order details. This information is used solely to process your request and is not shared with third parties.

We do not store payment card details. All payments are handled in person or through trusted payment gateways.

Terms of Service

  • Reservations must be cancelled at least 2 hours in advance or may be subject to a cancellation fee.
  • All prices are in Nepalese Rupees (NPR) and include applicable taxes where stated.
  • The restaurant reserves the right to refuse service at its discretion.
  • Outside food and beverages are not permitted.
  • Car wash services are subject to weather conditions and queue availability.

Refund Policy

Dine-in orders are non-refundable once prepared. If you experience a quality issue, please speak to the manager on duty immediately. Car wash refunds are handled case-by-case.
